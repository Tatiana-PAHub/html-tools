📋 Context & Outreach Summary

Strategy Overview: This video showcases a successful $7K retainer deal closed by a beginner who implemented a "Connector" business model. The approach involves:

  • Market Intelligence: Using Predict Leads to identify companies actively hiring (demand signal)
  • Targeted Outreach: Cold emailing both sides of the marketplace simultaneously
  • Matchmaking: Connecting logistics providers with defense/aerospace companies hiring
  • Infrastructure: Using Connector OS platform to automate and scale campaigns

Target Market: Defense logistics and aerospace companies experiencing growth due to geopolitical factors

Value Proposition: Connecting specialized logistics providers with companies actively hiring in operations/logistics

Success: $2,293/month for 3 months = ~$7K total retainer, closed within 4 days, no social proof needed

📧 Email Copy Templates

🎯 Demand-Side Email Copies

Outbound emails sent to companies actively hiring (demand side). These companies are the ones with open logistics/operations roles and need service providers.

Email 1: Kyle to Logistics Company 10/10 Rated
Kyle reached out because I saw a bunch of open roles on the ops side I V C across shipping and receiving, quality, customer support and related functions. I'm currently in conversations with logistics providers that can help when activity starts building across those areas at once. Would opening a conversation around that be worth it right now?
Email 2: Linda to Defense Company
Linda reached out because I noticed a defense company actively hiring for logistics roles or extending areas like procurement and improvement control. I'm looking to connect the right logistics partner when opportunities like this arise. Are you open to discussing this in the next 30-90 days?
Email 3: Milligros to Aerospace Company 10/10 Rated
Milligros reached out because I noticed a company in the aerospace sector actively hiring for logistics roles right now. They're looking for talents to support their mechanical and electro-automation needs in aerospace maintenance. I'm keen to connect logistics providers like you when opportunities arise. Are you open to exploring such conversations in the next 30-90 days?
Email 4: Kim to GE Space
Kim, saw you hiring for logistics roles at GE Space. I'm in touch with a few providers who support aerospace teams across logistics, procurement and program execution. Not sure if this is priority right now, but worth opening a conversation.

🤝 Supply-Side Email Copies

Outbound emails sent to service providers (supply side). These are logistics providers looking for new clients and opportunities.

They're hiring across shipping, quality, customer support and related operational functions. I'm looking to place the right logistics provider when situations like that show up.
